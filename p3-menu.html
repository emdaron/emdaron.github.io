OUR STORY

Palin is a graduate of the Culinary Program at Carlisle High school, and a graduate of the Penn College Culinary school, while also interning at Eagles Mere Country Club. He currently works at Momma Spriggs in Carlisle, PA.

Amy bakes at local fairs and competitions, starting in 2013. She has won First Place and Best of Show ribbons at local and county fairs, her biggest achievement currently being the Best of Show at the 2023 PA Farm Show.
