Step 2

You'll be using a "forward knot,‚Äù a simple knot where you make a "4" shape with the floss. Here's how to do it:

- Take the leftmost strand and lay it over the strand next to it, making a "4" shape.

- Pull the left strand under and through the hole at the bottom of the "4."

- Pull the left strand all the way through, tightening the knot.

- Repeat this process to make a knot all the way across the strands, always starting with the leftmost strand. Repeat with every thread.
