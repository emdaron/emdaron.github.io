TRAVEL THROUGH CENTRAL PENNSYLVANIA

ABOUT

Welcome to Central Pennsylvania, a region celebrated for its stunning landscapes and friendly communities. Known for its unique blend of small-town charm and vibrant culture, Central PA offers an ideal escape for those seeking adventure and connection. Explore the lush countryside and enjoy a variety of outdoor activities, while also taking the time to delve into the area's rich history. Discover fascinating stories from our past through historic sites and local museums that highlight the region's role in shaping the state. With lively seasonal festivals and a welcoming atmosphere, Central Pennsylvania invites you to create lasting memories and experience the depth of its heritage.

ATTRACTION OF THE WEEK: HERSHEY PARK

A renowned amusement park featuring exciting rides, live entertainment, and an opportunity to learn about chocolate-making at Hershey's Chocolate World.

CONNECT WITH NATURE

PA GRAND CANYON

Also known as Pine Creek Gorge, this natural wonder features stunning vistas, hiking trails, and opportunities for outdoor recreation.

PINE GROVE FURNACE STATE PARK

Serving as the "Gateway to the Appalachian Trail," this park offers hiking, picnicking, and swimming opportunities, along with historic features and natural beauty.

HAVE SOME FUN

Central Market York

The oldest continuously operating farmers' market in the U.S., offering a bustling atmosphere, local produce, crafts, and diverse food vendors.

LEWISBURG

A charming small town known for its historic downtown, offering boutique shops, galleries, restaurants, as well as attractions like the Lewisburg Farmers Market and Packwood House Museum.

LEARN OUR HISTORY

GETTYSBURG NATIONAL MILITARY PARK

Site of the pivotal Battle of Gettysburg during the Civil War, offering guided tours, historic monuments, and a visitor center with exhibits.

PA STATE CAPITOL

An impressive architectural gem in Harrisburg shocasing exquisite artwork, stunning interiors, and guided tours to learn about the state's government and history.
